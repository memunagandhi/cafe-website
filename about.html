Our Story

Brewing passion, serving community, and redefining elegance in Surat since 2024.

A Vision for Perfection.

It started with a simple idea: that coffee should be more than just a drink. It should be an experience. Founded by coffee enthusiasts in the heart of Surat, Maymuna's Cafe was born from a desire to bring world-class artisan standards to our beautiful city.

We spent months sourcing the perfect beans, perfecting our roasting profiles, and designing a space that feels like a sanctuary. Every piece of furniture, every light fixture, and every cup we serve is a reflection of our commitment to quality.

"Crafted with love, served with pride."
